# EasyRead

**Science papers in plain English — for everyone.**

EasyRead turns dense academic research into clear, jargon-free explanations that anyone can understand. Paste a paper abstract, get a plain-language version with real-world analogies within 24 hours — free during beta.

**Niches:** Medical / health research · Political science

🔗 **Live site:** [aakmedjonov-bot.github.io/EasyRead](https://aakmedjonov-bot.github.io/EasyRead)

---

## How to submit a paper

1. Go to the live site above
2. Paste your paper abstract or a link (PubMed, arXiv, bioRxiv, SSRN, medRxiv)
3. Leave your email
4. Receive a plain-language explanation within 24 hours

Open-access papers only. We don't process paywalled journals.

## Enabling GitHub Pages

1. Go to **Settings → Pages**
2. Under *Source*, select **Deploy from a branch**
3. Choose **main** branch, **/ (root)** folder
4. Save — your site will be live at `https://aakmedjonov-bot.github.io/EasyRead`
